Chair

The big rounded rectangular cat looking thing that I use as a profile picture everywhere. It has a history. Barely, though.

Drawing a seat

Early 2020, somewhere in February or March. I wanted to draw a chair.. thing. I opened GIMP, and drew a chair. Poorly. Very poorly.
To no surprise, I quickly got bored of staring at a "chair" and decided to add cat ears. Why that specifically? No reason. That's when I realized that it looks more like a cat than anything.
After a few changes, it became an actual cat looking thing; and best of all, it was my creation.

Rectangular cat looking thing

The more I looked at what I had created, the more I liked it. So I decided to adapt it as a profile picture, and that's when I felt like that image, that rectangular thing could definitely be my icon online. So I used it as that, on a lot of places online, never really thinking that I'd need something else. Not for a while, at least.

Chair: Remastered Edition

Somewhere in early-mid-2020, I cannot exactly remember when (apparently I uploaded it to my personal OneDrive in June 2020), I decided "screw it, I might as well try remaking Chair in a 2000px PNG file, that doesn't look like a crappy 500px MS Paint drawing". So I did that. And it's now been a year and a half, and I barely used anything else as a profile picture online.
This image is mine, I made it. Why use something else?

The Blinking Thing

I don't remember specifically what happened here. All I know is that some day I got bored and decided "im gonna animate chair in wick to make him blink".
So now there's this thing. It blinks. Cool. I like this thing. Sometimes I use it, but sometimes I don't because it makes performance crappy and some services just don't handle GIF files really well with profile pictures (example, Mastodon).
